Loan Application Process
Applying for a car loan involves the following steps:

1. Choose a lender and compare loan offers.

2. Submit required documents, including ID, proof of income, and address proof.

3. The lender assesses creditworthiness and verifies documents.

4. Approval or rejection is communicated within 5-7 business days.

5. If approved, the loan agreement is signed, and funds are disbursed.

6. Monthly repayments start as per the agreed schedule.
