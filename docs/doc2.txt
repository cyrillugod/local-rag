Interest Rates & Repayment Terms
Car loan interest rates vary based on credit score, loan tenure, and lender policies. Key points include:

Interest rates range from 5% to 15% annually.

Loan tenure options typically range from 12 to 84 months.

Fixed and variable interest rates are available.

Early repayment penalties may apply in some cases.

Monthly installments should not exceed 40% of the borrower's income.
